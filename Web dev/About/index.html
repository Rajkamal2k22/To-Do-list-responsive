ABOUT ME

I’m Rajkamal Kumar Singh, a passionate and driven student currently pursuing a B.Sc. IT degree at Patliputra University. As an aspiring software developer, I’m constantly expanding my skills and knowledge in the world of technology.

I’ve gained a solid foundation in C and C++, and I’m well-versed in web development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. Additionally, I have hands-on experience with DBMS (Database Management Systems), particularly with Oracle software.

Throughout my journey, I’ve worked on several personal projects, which include both web and software development. Whether it’s building interactive websites or creating programs in C and C++, I love bringing my ideas to life through code. Each project helps me learn new concepts, improve my problem-solving skills, and refine my technical abilities.

If you’re just starting out in coding or looking for new projects to work on, feel free to follow me on my official Instagram account, @code_maaster. On my Instagram, I regularly share tutorials, tips, and new projects that will help you grow your coding skills, along with resources for building your own web applications and more.

Looking ahead, I aim to specialize in software development and continue challenging myself with new projects while collaborating with like-minded individuals who share the same passion for technology.

Thank you for visiting my site! I hope you find my work inspiring and useful. Don’t hesitate to reach out for collaborations or if you’re curious about any of my projects.
